The argument
Hiring is a statement about the future. When you bring someone in full-time to manage your AI infrastructure, you're betting that function will always require a dedicated human. The best-run companies are making the opposite bet.
The goal isn't to staff a function — it's to build it, automate it, and move on. Starsight is designed for exactly that arc.
The traditional approach
A full-time role
Full-time roles naturally orient toward preservation. The systems stay manual, the knowledge stays siloed, and the org stays dependent on continued presence. It's structural, not personal.
