Common questions
Why isn't pricing on this page?

The audit is a fixed fee in a defined range, scaled to organization size and data complexity. We confirm the number on the intake call before you commit to anything. Quoting publicly would either underprice the larger engagements or overprice the smaller ones, and the goal of this page is to find the right fit, not the average one.

What time commitment do you actually need from us?

About three hours total from the budget owner across the week: a 90-minute kickoff Monday, a 60-minute review Thursday, and a 90-minute readout Friday. Plus four to five 30-minute interviews with stakeholders we identify on Monday. Everything else is on us.

How do you handle confidentiality and NDA?

Mutual NDA signed before kickoff. We hold an active TS/SCI clearance, which means data discipline is how we work, not an extra process to negotiate. The audit itself is unclassified work; the posture is the point.

Can we do equity instead of cash?

Not for the audit. Equity arrangements complicate fixed-fee work and put us on the wrong side of the incentive equation for an early engagement. If something larger emerges from the audit, equity can be on the table for the right fit.

How does the refund guarantee work?

If by Friday's readout you don't see a usable opportunity worth pursuing in the next 90 days, the engagement is refundable in full. The guarantee exists because the offer stakes our money on the work being useful, not just thorough.
