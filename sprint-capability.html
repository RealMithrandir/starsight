Taught by someone who's built AI systems for the DoD and the State Department.

This is not a certification program. It's not a vendor pitch disguised as training. It's a practitioner-led transfer of real judgment: how to evaluate what's worth building, what's not ready yet, and what questions to ask when someone tells you their AI product will solve your problem.

Your team leaves with a backlog they built themselves and a framework they can reuse. The goal is independence: after this workshop, your team makes better AI decisions without needing us in the room.
